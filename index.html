How It Works

  1. Set Up Your Restaurants: Add each location to your restaurants.json file, then run the Onest program (app.py) on your server or local machine.
  2. Generate & Print QR Codes: The system automatically creates unique QR codes for each restaurant. Print them and place them on tables, menus, or receipts.
  3. Customers Scan & Chat: Guests scan the QR code to access your feedback assistant (powered by GPT-4). They share their experiences in real time—no more stale surveys.
  4. Collect & Analyze: The app stores all conversations, then generates a detailed, AI-driven PDF report summarizing key insights, recurring themes, and actionable steps.
  5. Refine & Improve: Use your new, data-backed insights to delight customers, refine your service, and stay ahead of the competition.
