How It Works

  1. Set Up
    Chat with ONest to identify your business details, the aspects of your product/service you want to improve, and any specific questions you have for your customers.
  2. Generate QR Codes
    ONest creates QR codes and messaging to provide to your customers — on receipts, at the counter, or anywhere else they’ll see them.
  3. Customers Scan & Chat
    Customers scan a QR code with their phone and enter a voice or text chat with ONest (powered by OpenAI GPT-4). It adapts to their speaking style to put them at ease and get them talking about what matters.
  4. Understand Insights
    ONest finds patterns in the feedback and delivers insights in your preferred format — email, text, chat, you name it. You also get sources to original conversations for deeper context.
  5. Get Recommendations
    Receive tailored recommendations to improve your product/service, refine your customer questions, and continuously elevate future customer conversations.
  6. Adapt Your Interviews
    Just say the word and ONest will update future interviews to include your new insights and directions.
